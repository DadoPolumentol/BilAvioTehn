Суштина каљења састоји се у томе да се челик: 

најпре загреје до одређене температуре, а затим наглим хлађењем преводи у жељену структуру. На тај начин добија

Легуре алуминијума које су термики обрађене имају ознаку?

W, T, T(2-10)


By heat treatment in a wider context we mean a process by which

by thermal action a certain change of the material in solid state is achieved, so that the material obtains the desired structure and mechanical properties.


Thermochemical treatment consists of the following:

certain elements — such as carbon, nitrogen, chromium, silicon, etc. — are introduced into the surface of steel by diffusion. In this case the chemical composition also changes.


The essence of hardening (quenching) КАЉЕЊЕ is that steel

is first heated to a certain temperature, then rapidly cooled to transform it into the desired structure. In this way the steel gains the highest possible hardness.


Heat-treated aluminum alloys carry the designation:

W, T, T(2–10).
