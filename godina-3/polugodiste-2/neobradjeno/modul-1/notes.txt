Integers or signed whole numbers - all positive and negative whole numbers including 0

Product - result of multiplication

Dividend / divisor = quotient

Multiple - a number that is divisible by another number is the multiple of that number
10 / 2 = 5
10 - multiple of 2
multiple / n = number

Factor - a number that another number is divisible by
10 / 2 = 5
2 - factor of 10
factor * n = number

Lowest common multiple - smallest positive number which is a multiple of two or more numbers
LCM(12,80)=?
12=2*2*3
80=2*5*2*2*2
LCM(12,80)=2*2*3*5*2*2=240

Highest common factor - biggest number that will divide two or more numbers
HCF(240,924)=?
240=2*5*2*2*2*3
924=2*2*3*7*11
HCF(240,924)=2*2*3=12

Prime number - only divisible by itself and 1

Reciprocal - reciprocal of a number is = 1 / number

Numerator - top part of fraction
1/2
1 - numerator

Denominator - bottom part of a fraction
1/2
2 - denominator

Proper fraction - numerator < denominator

Improper fraction - numerator >= denominator

Mixed number - whole number and fraction
1½

Power / exponent / indecie - x^y
y - power
x - base

Equilateral triangle - 3 equal sides
Isoceles triangle - 2 equal sides
Scalene triangle - 3 different sides

Acute triangle - all the angles < 90°
Right triangle - one angle = 90°
Obtuse triangle - one angle > 90°

Parallelogram - surface A=ah

Trapezoid - surface A=((a_1+a_2)/2)*h

Circle - 
circumference = 2rπ
Area = (r^2)π

Elipse - 
Circumference = 2π\sqrt{\frac{a^2+b^2}{2}}
Area = abπ
a - r_1
b - r_2
